Research Focus

Nanostructures, chiral interactions, and AI-driven simulation.

Our lab investigates magnetism and magnetic dynamics in restricted-symmetry systems, including confined nanostructures, low-dimensional materials, and systems with inversion symmetry breaking. These platforms reveal rich phenomena driven by anisotropy, dipolar interactions, and Dzyaloshinskii-Moriya interaction.

We combine Monte Carlo methods, Langevin dynamics, and Landau-Lifshitz simulations, and integrate deep learning workflows to accelerate structure discovery and topological analysis. We collaborate with partners at Fudan University and the SPLEEM group at LBNL.

Core Directions

Chiral Magnetism

Skyrmions, domain walls, and DMI-engineered spin textures.

Computational Physics

Monte Carlo and spin dynamics for finite and low-dimensional systems.

AI for Materials

Deep learning for classification, generation, and topological indexing.
